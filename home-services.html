Simple Process

How to Book a Service

From booking to pickup — we keep it simple so you can focus on riding.

Step 01

Book Online

Choose your service type, pick a date and time slot, describe the issue, and confirm your booking in under 3 minutes.

Step 02

We Diagnose & Update

Drop off your bike. Our mechanic runs a full diagnostic and sends you a real-time update with the findings and cost estimate before touching a single bolt.

Step 03

Pick Up & Ride

We notify you the moment your bike is ready. Pick it up, pay in-store or online, and get a full breakdown of what was done — plain English, no jargon.
