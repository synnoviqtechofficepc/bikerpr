Our Story

Built By Riders,
For Riders

VeloWorks started in 2010 as a one-mechanic operation out of a converted garage on Spoke Lane. Founder Marcus Chen had a simple idea: a bike shop that treated every customer like a fellow cyclist, not just a sale.

Today, we're a team of 14 passionate mechanics and staff who collectively have over 80 years of riding experience — road, mountain, gravel, and everything in between.
