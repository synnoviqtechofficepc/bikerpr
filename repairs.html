Repair Services

What We Fix

From a simple flat to a full competition overhaul — our certified mechanics handle it all.

Drivetrain Service

Chain replacement, cassette swap, derailleur adjustment and cable replacement, front and rear. Includes full clean and lubrication.

From $45 labour

Brake System Overhaul

Cable, housing, and pad replacement for mechanical brakes. Hydraulic bleed, hose replacement, and rotor truing for disc systems.

From $30 per wheel

Wheel Service

True and tension adjustment, spoke replacement, rim tape, tubeless setup, tyre fitting, valve replacement.

From $20 per wheel

Flat Tyre Repair

Tube replacement, tyre inspection for embedded debris, thorn-resistant liners fitted on request. Road-side or workshop.

From $18 incl. tube

Full Overhaul

Complete disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, bearing overhaul, cable replacement, re-grease, full reassembly and precision adjustment.

From $120 labour

Electronic Shifting

Di2, AXS, Campagnolo EPS calibration, firmware update, junction box service, battery replacement. Bosch & Shimano eMTB systems.

From $65 per session
