Our Story

From a Garage to Portland's Most Trusted Shop

In 2010, Marcus Chen converted a two-car garage on Spoke Lane into a one-man repair shop. His tools were second-hand. His espresso machine was not. He had one rule: treat every bike as if it were your own.

Word spread fast. Within two years, VeloWorks had moved into its current 4,000 sq ft home, and the team had grown to six mechanics — each a passionate rider in their own right.

Today we're 14 strong, serving over 4,200 active members, maintaining a 35-bike rental fleet, and hosting monthly community rides. The garage is gone. The rule never changed.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Three things guide every decision we make at VeloWorks — from how we wrench to how we talk to customers.

Radical Honesty

We'll never recommend a service your bike doesn't need. Our diagnostic is always free, and the quote is always fixed before any work begins. No surprises on pickup.

Technical Excellence

Every mechanic holds certification from at least one major brand. We invest in ongoing training, the right tools, and the time to do each job properly — not quickly.

Local Commitment

1% of our gross revenue goes to maintaining Portland-area trails. We partner with Cycle Oregon and local land trusts to keep the places we love rideable.
